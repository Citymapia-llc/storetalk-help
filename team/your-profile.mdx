---
title: "Updating your profile and password"
description: "Change your display name or update your password from Settings."
---

Every team member — including the Owner — can update their own display name and password from **Settings → Profile**. Your email address is set when you join and cannot be changed.

## Changing your display name

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">Click **Settings** in the left menu.</Step>
  <Step title="Open the Profile tab">Click the **Profile** tab (the first tab).</Step>
  <Step title="Edit your name">Update the Display Name field.</Step>
  <Step title="Save">Click **Save** — the change takes effect immediately.</Step>
</Steps>

## Changing your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Profile">Click **Settings**, then the **Profile** tab.</Step>
  <Step title="Find the password section">Scroll to the **Change Password** section.</Step>
  <Step title="Enter your current password">Type your existing password to confirm it's you.</Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new password">Minimum 8 characters. Confirm it in the next field.</Step>
  <Step title="Update">Click **Update Password**.</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Forgotten your current password? Sign out and use the **Forgot password** link on the login page instead.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Your email address cannot be changed — it is used to verify your identity and is tied to your invite. Contact the store Owner if you need to switch to a different email.
</Warning>
