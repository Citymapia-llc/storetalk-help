title: "Team roles explained"
description: "What each role can see and do in StoreTalk."
---

Every team member has a role that controls which parts of StoreTalk they can access. There are four roles: **Owner**, **Admin**, **Manager**, and **Agent**.

## Role overview

| Role | Who it is for | What they can access |
|------|--------------|----------------------|
| Owner | The business owner — one per account | Everything, including billing, WhatsApp settings, and team management |
| Admin | A trusted manager or operations lead | Everything except billing and WhatsApp number settings |
| Manager | A team lead who handles customers and pipeline | Inbox, Contacts, Segments, and Pipeline — no templates or campaigns |
| Agent | A customer support or sales executive | Inbox, Dashboard, and Help only |

<Tip>
  Start new hires as Agent and promote them to Manager or Admin as they get familiar with the platform.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Only the Owner can change WhatsApp settings, manage billing, or invite Admins. There can only be one Owner per account.
</Warning>
