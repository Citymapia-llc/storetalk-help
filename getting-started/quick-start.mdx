---

Follow these five steps to get StoreTalk working for your business.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your WhatsApp number">
    Go to **Settings → WhatsApp** and follow the Embedded Signup flow to connect your WhatsApp Business number. You will need to log in to your Facebook Business account during this step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Knowledge Base">
    Go to **Knowledge Base** in the left menu and upload your product catalogue or FAQ document. Plain text (.txt) or Markdown (.md) files work best.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hire your first AI staff member">
    Go to **AI Agents → Browse AI Staff Library** and choose a role. The **Sales Executive** is the most popular first hire — it handles product enquiries and lead qualification automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send yourself a test message">
    Send a WhatsApp message to your business number. Your AI staff member will reply automatically using your knowledge base.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Inbox">
    Open the **Inbox** in StoreTalk. You will see the conversation appear in real time. From here your team can take over if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start with just one AI staff member. Once you're comfortable with how it works, you can hire more specialised roles for support, sales, and order follow-ups.
</Tip>
