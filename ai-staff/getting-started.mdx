---
title: "Getting started with AI Staff"
description: "Hire your first AI staff member in 5 steps."
---

AI Staff are pre-built expert roles you hire and deploy to your WhatsApp channel. Each staff member handles a specific topic using your knowledge base.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to AI Agents">
    Click **AI Agents** in the left sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the AI Staff Library">
    Click **Browse AI Staff Library** to see all available roles.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a role">
    Pick a role that matches your business need. The **Sales Executive** handles product enquiries and lead qualification — it's the most popular first hire.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the 5-step setup">
    The setup wizard will walk you through: knowledge base → name & language → dos & don'ts → model → agreement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to your team">
    Click **Add to My Team** — your new staff member is live immediately on your WhatsApp number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The Sales Executive is the most popular first hire — it handles product enquiries and lead qualification automatically.
</Tip>

## Available roles

| Role               | Best for                                          |
|--------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Sales Executive    | Product questions, pricing, lead qualification    |
| Support Executive  | Policy questions, returns, order issues           |
| Order Follow-up    | Proactive updates on pending or shipped orders    |
| Appointment Setter | Booking slots and confirming schedules            |
