StoreTalk offers three plans: **Starter** (free), **Growth**, and **Pro**. Each plan includes a different number of AI staff members and monthly conversation volume.

## Plan comparison

| Feature | Starter | Growth | Pro |
|---------|---------|--------|-----|
| AI staff members included | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| Monthly AI conversations | 500 | 2,000 | 10,000 |
| Team members | 3 | 10 | Unlimited |
| Pipelines | 1 | 3 | Unlimited |
| Campaigns | 2/month | 10/month | Unlimited |
| WhatsApp templates | 5 | 25 | Unlimited |

## AI Staff add-ons

You can add individual AI staff members at **₹499/month each** as add-ons to any paid plan.

<Tip>
  Start on Starter and upgrade when you hit the conversation limit. Most growing businesses move to Growth within the first 3 months.
</Tip>

## How usage is counted

<Warning>
  AI staff conversations count toward your monthly usage limit. Check your current usage in **Settings → Billing**.
</Warning>

A "conversation" is counted each time a customer starts a new WhatsApp thread with your AI staff. Follow-up messages within the same 24-hour window do not count as a new conversation.

## Checking your usage

Go to **Settings → Billing** to see:

- Current plan and renewal date
- Conversations used this month vs. your limit
- Add-on AI staff members
- Invoice history

## Upgrading your plan

Click **Upgrade** in **Settings → Billing**. The new plan takes effect immediately and you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the month.
