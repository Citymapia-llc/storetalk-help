## What is StoreTalk?

StoreTalk connects your WhatsApp Business number to a powerful automation platform. You can:

- **Answer customer questions automatically** using AI staff trained on your products and policies
- **Send campaigns** to hundreds of customers at once using approved WhatsApp templates
- **Manage conversations** from a shared team inbox — no more forwarding WhatsApp messages
- **Track your sales pipeline** and manage customer contacts in one place
