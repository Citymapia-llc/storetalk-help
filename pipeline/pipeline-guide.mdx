## Setting up your pipeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pipeline">Click **Pipeline** in the left menu.</Step>
  <Step title="Create your first pipeline">Click **New Pipeline** and give it a name (e.g. "Main Sales Pipeline").</Step>
  <Step title="Add your stages">These are the steps in your sales process вҖ” for example: New Lead вҶ’ Contacted вҶ’ Demo вҶ’ Proposal вҶ’ Won.</Step>
  <Step title="Save">Your empty board is ready.</Step>
</Steps>

## Adding a lead

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a card">Click the **+** button under any stage column.</Step>
  <Step title="Find the contact">Search for the contact by name or phone number.</Step>
  <Step title="Choose their stage">Select which stage they are currently in.</Step>
  <Step title="Add notes">Write any context (e.g. "Interested in 50 units, needs invoice").</Step>
  <Step title="Save">The contact now appears as a card on your board.</Step>
</Steps>

## Moving a lead forward

When a lead progresses, drag their card to the next column. The system records how many days they spent in each stage.

<Tip>
  The "days in stage" badge tells you if a lead has gone cold. If someone has been in "Contacted" for 7+ days without moving forward, it's time to follow up.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  The Starter plan supports 1 pipeline. Upgrade to Growth or Pro to create multiple pipelines for different products or teams.
</Warning>
