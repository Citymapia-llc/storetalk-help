This section is for StoreTalk Consultants — partners who help Indian businesses set up, configure, and get the most out of StoreTalk.

## What consultants do

A StoreTalk Consultant typically:

- **Sets up the initial account** — WABA connection, templates, WhatsApp number configuration
- **Configures AI staff** — knowledge base creation, persona tuning, dos & don'ts
- **Trains the business team** — walks the store owner and staff through the platform
- **Provides ongoing support** — monitors performance and fine-tunes AI responses

## Consultant vs store owner access

As a consultant, you will typically be added to the client's StoreTalk account as an **Admin**. This gives you access to everything except billing and WhatsApp number settings (which require Owner access).

For WABA setup and phone provisioning, you will need temporary Owner-level access or to work alongside the store owner directly.
